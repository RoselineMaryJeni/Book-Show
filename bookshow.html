Hamlet

William Shakespeare

Prince Hamlet of Denmark is deeply troubled by the sudden death of his father, King Hamlet, and the hasty remarriage of his mother, Queen Gertrude, to his uncle, Claudius. One night, the ghost of King Hamlet appears to Prince Hamlet and reveals that he was murdered by Claudius. The ghost urges Hamlet to avenge his death.

Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen

Pride and Prejudice, romantic novel by Jane Austen, published anonymously in three volumes in 1813. A classic of English literature, written with incisive wit and superb character delineation, it centers on the burgeoning relationship between Elizabeth Bennet, the daughter of a country gentleman, and Fitzwilliam Darcy, a rich aristocratic landowner.
